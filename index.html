Climate Change Awareness

Protecting our planet for our future

What Causes Climate Change?

Industrial Pollution

Burning fossil fuels releases carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gases into the atmosphere.

Deforestation

Cutting down forests reduces carbon absorption, increasing CO₂ levels in the atmosphere.

Melting Ice Caps

Rising temperatures lead to melting glaciers and rising sea levels, endangering habitats.

Effects of Climate Change

  • Extreme weather events such as hurricanes, droughts, and floods.
  • Rising sea levels leading to displacement of coastal communities.
  • Loss of biodiversity due to habitat destruction.
  • Increase in wildfires and temperature extremes.

How You Can Help

  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle to minimize waste.
  • Use energy-efficient appliances and renewable energy sources.
  • Plant trees and support reforestation programs.
  • Raise awareness and advocate for climate policies.
